PetalData Pricing

PetalData is free to use when downloading less than 10,000 rows. Beyond 10,000 rows, PetalData's pricing is based on the max number of rows downloaded per-request:

  • < 10k rows=Free
  • < 2 mil rows=$99/mo
  • < 5 mil rows=$199/mo
  • < 12 mil rows=$499/mo

Email support@petaldata.app to obtain an API key.
